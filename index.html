Children of Kerala

A Kent based charity

Children of Kerala provides life's basics and essential equipment for under privileged and disabled children living in the rural Kerala region of southern India.

Started in 2005, by our founder Elaine Craven MBE, we have raised thousands of pounds and helped hundreds of children in specialist schools, orphanages and communities.

Every penny we raise goes towards helping the children.

Being a small charity run by volunteers, we are able to make sure every single penny of our sponsor donations and proceeds from charity events go directly to helping the children in Kerala. All travel to Kerala, expenses, stationery, raffle prizes and everything that makes the charity tick are either donated by local organisations or paid for by the charity volunteers.
